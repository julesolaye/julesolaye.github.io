Welcome!

My name is Jules Olayé. I am a Ph.D. student at Ecole Polytechnique, Palaiseau, France, working under the supervision of Marie Doumic and Milica Tomašević. I am interested in the study of mathematical models coming from biology, with both stochastic and deterministic approaches.

Research topics

  • Asymptotic analysis of branching processes,
  • Inverse problems,
  • Cell dynamics,
  • Applications to biology.
