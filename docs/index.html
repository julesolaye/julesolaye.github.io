Welcome!

My name is Jules Olayé. I am a Ph.D. student at Ecole Polytechnique, Palaiseau, France, working under the supervision of Marie Doumic and Milica Tomašević. I am interested in the study of mathematical models coming from biology, with both deterministic and stochastic approaches.

Research topics

  • Integro-differential equations,
  • Age-dependent branching processes,
  • Density estimation,
  • Applications to biology.
